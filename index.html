Welcome to Achham

Nestled in the far-western region of Nepal, Achham is a land of breathtaking natural beauty, ancient temples, and rich cultural heritage. With its pristine lakes, verdant hills, and traditional villages, it offers an authentic glimpse into Nepal's unspoiled countryside.

From the mesmerizing Ramaroshan lakes to the historic Mangalsen Fort and sacred Baidyanath Temple, Achham invites you to explore its hidden treasures and immerse yourself in its unique traditions and warm hospitality.

Join us on a journey to discover one of Nepal's best-kept secrets, where nature, culture, and spirituality blend harmoniously to create unforgettable experiences.

Featured Attractions

Discover the natural wonders and cultural treasures that make Achham a must-visit destination

Ramaroshan Lakes

A breathtaking complex of 12 pristine lakes surrounded by lush forests and rolling hills. This natural paradise offers spectacular hiking trails, diverse wildlife, and stunning panoramic views that will leave you in awe.

Mangalsen Fort

Explore the historic ruins of this ancient fort that once served as the capital of the Khasa Kingdom. Perched on a hilltop, it offers fascinating insights into the region's rich history and commanding views of the surrounding valleys.

Baidyanath Dham

Visit this sacred temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, one of the most important pilgrimage sites in the region. The ancient architecture, spiritual atmosphere, and traditional rituals offer a glimpse into Nepal's rich religious heritage.
