How to Reach Achham

From Kathmandu

  • Fly to Dhangadhi Airport (1 hour) + 3-hour scenic drive
  • Tourist bus journey (12-14 hours) - comfortable overnight option
  • Private vehicle hire (10-12 hours) - flexibility to stop

From Nepalgunj

  • Direct bus service (6-7 hours) - most economical
  • Shared jeep (5-6 hours) - faster alternative
  • Local flights available during peak season
💡 Pro Tip

Book transport in advance during festival seasons (September-November). Road conditions are best from October to April.

Local Etiquette & Culture

✓ Do's

  • Greet with "Namaste" with palms together
  • Remove shoes before entering homes/temples
  • Dress modestly, especially at religious sites
  • Ask permission before photographing people
  • Use right hand for giving/receiving items

✗ Don'ts

  • Don't touch someone's head or point feet at people
  • Don't eat beef or touch leather items near temples
  • Don't show affection publicly
  • Don't waste food - it's considered disrespectful
  • Don't ignore local customs during festivals

Seasonal Weather Info

🌨️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

5°C - 18°C

Cold mornings, clear skies. Perfect for trekking with proper gear.

🌸 Spring (Mar-May)

12°C - 25°C

Best time to visit! Pleasant weather, blooming rhododendrons.

☔ Monsoon (Jun-Sep)

18°C - 28°C

Heavy rainfall, lush greenery. Roads may be challenging.

🍂 Autumn (Oct-Nov)

8°C - 22°C

Excellent visibility, festival season, ideal for photography.

🌟 Best Time to Visit

October to April offers the most favorable conditions with clear skies, comfortable temperatures, and accessible roads. Spring (March-May) is particularly beautiful with blooming flowers.
